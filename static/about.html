Who Are We?

SocketBox is a push notification service which allows developers to send real time data in terms of notifications to your applications from a server .

SocketBox is developed by 2 Computer Science Engineers who ealized the importance and the need for a push notification service while working on another project and decided to simplify the whole process by building a separate , simple and easy to use push notification service for developers.
